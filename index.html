Biography

LinkedIn

I received BS and MS degrees in Electronic Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2010 and 2013, respectively. I also received MS in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the Georgia Institute of Technology in 2013. From 2013 to 2017, I worked with Prof. Robert W. Heath Jr. on millimeter-wave communications. After receiving my Ph.D. degree from the University of Texas at Austin in 2017, I joined Samsung Research America.

Research

Google Scholar, Semantic Scholar, dblp, WebofScience, GitHub, ORCID: 0000-0003-1877-2730

From 2009 to 2013, my research interest was in physical layer security in wireless communication (Supervisor: Prof. Meixia Tao). From 2013 to 2017, I worked with Prof. Robert W. Heath, Jr. on millimeter-wave communications, focusing on few-bit ADC. I am now a staff engineer with Samsung Research America. My current work is around 5G standards and commercialization, including wireless self-backhaul, and UE/BS beam codebook design.
